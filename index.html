Future Projects

Magnet Powered Rockets

Now-Summer 2020

Recently a few new ideas based on areospace concepts have crossed my mind. One of those being a rocket utilising the power of magnets. Though this may not be at the level to launch a rocketship all the way to space, I was thinking it could reduce the amount of rocket-fuel needed to launch a rocket. We are nearing a time where we are concerned about the amount of fuel avaliable for humanity to use. So any changes to the amount of fuel needed for something as big as a rocket and something as costly as rocket-fuel could make a huge difference. I am working on making this idea a reality and possible my next science fair project.
